Kute Family Tree

History

Kute family is a prominent family from Vidharbh region of Maharashtra. Kute family belongs to Tirale Kunbi caste of Hindu religion. Their mother tongue is Marathi.

The village called Warud from Darwha taluka of Yavatmal district was established by a person from Kute family many years ago. Even today more than 50% residents of Warud are KUTE.

It is said that these people migrated from the region near today's Malegaon taluka from Washim district. There are still villages like 'Pangri Kute', 'Davha Kute' near Malegaon town where most of the people have surname Kute. Malegaon has a special area called 'Kute Chawl'. As their caste is same as Kute's from Warud, they can be called distant relatives of each other.

Previous generations

The first known name in the history of Kute family is Balaji Kute. If we assume each generation gap to be on an average of 25 years. He must have been born between 1700 AD and 1750 AD. Following are the generations that followed:

  1. Krushnaji Balaji Kute
  2. Raghoji Krushnaji Kute
  3. Yadavji Raghoji Kute
  4. Pralhad Yadavji Kute
  5. Shamji Pralhad Kute

First generation

Shamji Kute had two sons:

  1. Gabuji Shamji Kute
  2. Suryabhanji Shamji Kute

Out of these two, all the generations after Suryabhanji are known till date. All these generations are explained in detail down below.

Gabuji Kute had three children: Hiraji, Isramji and Raoji. Generations after Raoji that are known are as follows:

  1. Vithoba Raoji Kute
  2. Kacharaji Vithoba Kute
  3. Junaji Vithoba Kute

Junaji Kute is alive and lives with his family in Walsa, Taluka-Darwha, District-Yavatmal.
